Our Story

Bold Flavours.
Honest Food.

Tucked inside City Tower on Mosley Street, Do Eat brings Manchester the vibrant, comforting flavours of authentic Chinese and Asian cooking — no frills, no fuss, just brilliant food at honest prices.

We specialise in handcrafted ramen broths, silky noodle dishes and hearty rice plates built on time-honoured recipes. Every bowl is made with care, using premium ingredients and daily-made sauces that let the flavour do the talking. Whether you're grabbing a solo lunch or settling in for dinner with friends, Do Eat is your spot.

From the exterior of our City Tower kitchen to the plates we serve, quality is the promise behind every dish.
